  • About me

Eric Lopez is a pictorial artist who lives and works from Redwood City, California. Acquiring a strong passion for art from an early age, he has developed skills in various mediums, including charcoal, graphite, pastel, acrylic, and oil.

He studied illustration for a short time at the Academy of Art in San Francisco before graduating from the College of San Mateo, where he earned an AA degree in Studio Art and Art History.

From beautiful landscapes, flowers, still life, to portraits his artworks span different intriguing subjects. His attention to detail and use of deep rich colors brings his paintings to life.
